# Code of Conduct

We are committed to providing a welcoming and harassment-free environment for everyone.

## Our Standards
- Be respectful, inclusive, and considerate in all interactions.
- Provide and gracefully accept constructive feedback.
- Focus on what is best for the community and users.
- Use welcoming and inclusive language.

## Unacceptable Behavior
- Harassment, discrimination, or derogatory comments.
- Trolling, insulting, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Any conduct that would be inappropriate in a professional setting.

## Enforcement
- Report incidents privately to **security@confidentialcomputinglab.local**.
- Maintainers will review and respond promptly, respecting confidentiality and documenting outcomes.
- Consequences may include warnings, temporary bans, or permanent removal from the community depending on severity.

By participating, you agree to abide by this Code of Conduct.
